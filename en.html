Privacy Policy

Application: License Museum
Last updated: June 30, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how user information is handled in the app "License Museum" (the "App"). By using the App, you are deemed to have agreed to this Policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not ask you to directly provide personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number. No account registration or login is required.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The qualifications you register (name, exam date, application period, result-announcement date, target study time, memo, status, etc.), your study logs, the display name you enter, and your settings (sound, vibration, and notification on/off, Pomodoro durations, etc.) are stored only on your device. This information is never sent to our servers. Deleting the App also removes this information from your device (there is no cloud sync or backup).

4. Advertising (Third-Party Data Collection)

The App displays advertisements using "Google AdMob," an ad-serving service provided by Google. AdMob may automatically collect and use the following information to deliver, measure, and optimize ads:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA)
  • Device information such as device type and OS version
  • Usage data such as ad impressions and taps

For how this information is handled, please review Google's privacy policy.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Ad personalization settings: https://adssettings.google.com

5. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS, a dialog requesting permission to track may appear when advertising identifiers are used to optimize ads. You may choose "Ask App Not to Track," and the App will continue to function (showing non-personalized ads).

6. Rewarded Ads

The App lets you optionally watch video (rewarded) ads to receive benefits such as unlocking display shelves. Watching is entirely optional, and the App's core features remain available without doing so.

7. Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you of events such as application opening, application deadlines, exam dates, and result announcements. All notifications are generated and scheduled on your device, and no information is sent to external servers. You can turn notifications off at any time from within the App's settings or from the iOS "Settings" app.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for educational and productivity purposes and is suitable for a wide range of ages. If used by children under 13, we recommend use under the supervision of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Disclosure to Third Parties

Except for the advertising provider described above (Google), we do not sell or provide user information to third parties.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be changed without prior notice due to legal changes or new App features. Any changes take effect when posted on this page.
